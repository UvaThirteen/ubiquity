Meet the Band

Isaac Tapia

Drums • Vocals

Percussion master and the heartbeat of the band. Brings explosive energy and soulful harmonies.

Isaac played drums growing up until age 7–8 and returned in May 2023 at age 26.
"My playing style brings a hip-hop bass drum with rock and pop grooves that sounds kind of jazzy. Holds the groove."

Elijah Tapia

Guitar • Vocals

Lead guitarist and primary songwriter. Crafts infectious riffs and soaring melodies.

Started guitar in January 2024. Inspired by 80s & 90s R&B. Always loved to sing.
"My playing style is derived from clean R&B rhythms and some funk/Latin tunes. We all build off each other and let everyone shine."

Isaiah Tapia

Bass • Vocals

Low-end specialist. Drives the funk with pocket-deep grooves and rich vocal layers.

Started bass in January 2024. Passion from funk and soul.
"My playing style is derived from funk rhythms and Latin melodies to create a hyphy-punk sound."
